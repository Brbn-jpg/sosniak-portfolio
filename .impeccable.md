## Design Context

### Users
Recruiters, HR managers, and senior executives in the IVD (In Vitro Diagnostics) and medical device industry. They are looking for professional competence, leadership experience, and technical understanding.

### Brand Personality
**Clinical High-Performance**: Professional, precise, authoritative, yet innovative. The interface should feel like a high-end medical device or a premium business consultancy.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Minimalist Precision**: Clean lines, a "scientific" grid, high-contrast typography (serif/sans-serif pairing), and subtle animations that convey speed and accuracy. Emerald (brand color) used as a precision accent against a deep navy background.

### Design Principles
1. **Precision first**: Every element should feel aligned and intentional. No "slop" or random gaps.
2. **Speed of Information**: Key stats and the value proposition (hero text) must be immediately legible and impact-driven.
3. **Subtle Sophistication**: Use motion to reveal depth (parallax) without distracting from the content.
4. **Authoritative Clarity**: Bold typography and clean layouts that reflect senior leadership.
